# Product

An AI SEO checker: a web app where users submit content (copy, posts, pages) and
the tool analyzes it for search-engine optimization, then returns concrete,
explainable fixes.

## Problem

Marketing and content teams ship content that underperforms in search because SEO
review is manual, inconsistent, and easy to skip. The tool makes SEO feedback fast,
consistent, and actionable so non-experts can self-correct before publishing.

## Target users

Marketing and content teams (in-house marketers, social/content managers) who
write or own content but are not dedicated SEO specialists.

## What a demo must show

- Submit a piece of content and get an SEO assessment back.
- The assessment is specific and explainable (what is wrong, why, how to fix),
  not a vague score.
- AI is aimed at the right problem and its output is checked, not trusted blindly.

## Unknowns (confirm as scope firms up)

- Input surface: paste text, upload, or fetch a live URL.
- Which SEO dimensions are in scope for the demo (keywords, meta tags,
  readability, headings/structure, internal links) and which are deferred.
- Whether a target keyword/topic is user-provided or inferred.
- Persistence: whether analyses are saved or one-shot.
